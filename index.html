About Me

Hi! I'm Yifan Wang, a second year master student in the Language Science and Technology Department (LST) at Saarlang University. Currently I'm working as a research assistant under Prof. Dr. Vera Demberg. Prior to this, I got my Bachelor degree in Germanistics at Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2020.

My currrent research interests mainly involve identifying and correcting some undesired behaviors in NLP systems. For example, I have been working on mitigating gender biases in pre-trained models and identifying inconsistency in summarization systems. I'm also discovering new research directions that I find interesting and meaningful for AI development.

In my free time, I enjoy walking around in the city. As probably the worst cook in the world, I'm always willing to join a food adventure.
